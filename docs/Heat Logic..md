### Heat Status Logig

The heat status logic is used to determine the current state of a heating system based on various inputs such as temperature readings, user settings, and system conditions. The logic typically involves the following states:


### DRAFT - The initial state where the heat has not yet started.
### ACTIVE - The heat is ready for marshalling, and participants can start gathering.
### MARSHALLING - The process of collecting all participants for the heat is underway.
### READY - All participants are marshalled, and the heat is ready to start.
### JUDGING - The heat is currently in progress, and performances are being judged.
### REVIEWING - The heat has completed, and the results are being reviewed by the scrutineer.
### CHECKING - The results have been reviewed by the chairperson and are ready for final checking.
### COMPLETE - The heat is complete, and the results are final.
### CANCELED - The heat has been canceled and will not proceed.
